UNIVERSITY OF NAIROBI

Introduction to Robotics

Course Summary

Lecturer

Prof. James Kimani

Duration

12-16 weeks

Study Time

4-6 hours per week

Course Fee

Free

Course Overview

This comprehensive course introduces students to the fascinating world of robotics, covering fundamental concepts, programming, and hands-on applications.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand basic robotics principles and mechanics
  • Learn robot programming using Python and ROS
  • Gain hands-on experience with robot assembly
  • Develop problem-solving skills in robotics applications

Prerequisites

Basic understanding of mathematics and programming concepts. No prior robotics experience required.

Assessment

Continuous assessment through practical assignments (60%), mid-term exam (20%), and final project (20%).
